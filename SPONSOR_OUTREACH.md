# IRMAA Check Sponsor Outreach

## Positioning

IRMAA Check reaches people researching Medicare premium surcharges before or during retirement income decisions. The best sponsor fit is educational, planning-oriented, and aligned with Roth conversions, RMDs, taxable income events, Medicare planning, or advisor-client education.

Use this short pitch:

```text
IRMAA Check helps adults estimate how Roth conversions, RMDs, capital gains, home sales, and other income events may affect future Medicare premiums. We are opening clearly labeled educational sponsorship placements for companies serving retirement income, tax-planning, Medicare education, and financial advisor audiences.
```

## Rules

- Keep sponsors clearly labeled as Advertisement.
- Do not imply tax, legal, investment, Medicare enrollment, or insurance advice.
- Prefer educational sponsor assets: checklists, guides, webinars, calculators, planning explainers.
- Avoid Medicare Advantage or insurance-plan marketing until compliance review is stronger.
