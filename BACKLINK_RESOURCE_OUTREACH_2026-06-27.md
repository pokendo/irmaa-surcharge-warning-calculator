# IRMAA Check Backlink and Resource Outreach Plan - June 27, 2026

## Purpose

This is the authority-building sprint for IRMAA Check.

The goal is not to sell sponsor ads yet. The goal is to earn relevant links, citations, resource mentions, and newsletter/social mentions from retirement planning, Medicare education, tax planning, advisor, and personal finance sites.

Search Console is already showing that Google understands the topic, but the domain needs more trust. Backlinks and resource mentions are the next lever.

## Outreach Rules

- Do not ask for paid links.
- Do not mass-send the same email.
- Do not pitch direct competitors as if they owe us a link.
- Prioritize helpful updates, resource-page inclusion, calculator mentions, and newsletter tips.
- Lead with a specific reason their readers would benefit.
- Use one page-specific link, not five links.
- If the site sells Medicare plans or insurance leads, avoid anything that sounds like Medicare plan marketing.
- Keep the ask low-pressure: "If useful for your readers" or "possible resource for an update."
