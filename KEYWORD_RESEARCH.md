## Initial Article Priorities

1. Roth conversion and IRMAA: strongest sponsor alignment and high user intent.
2. RMDs and IRMAA: recurring annual planning problem for Medicare-age readers.
3. Capital gains and IRMAA: common surprise income event with calculator fit.

## Article Template

Each longtail article should include:

- Short answer in the first screen.
- Plain-English explanation of the income event.
- Link to the calculator near the top and again after the main explanation.
- Example scenario with estimated monthly and annual surcharge impact.
- Reminder that 2026 IRMAA uses 2024 MAGI.
- Sponsor slot after the first useful answer, not before it.
- Related links to Medicare MAGI, 2026 brackets, and SSA-44 where relevant.
