# IRMAA Calculator Competitor Analysis

Reviewed: June 1, 2026

## Top Competitor Set

1. Enroll in Medicare IRMAA Calculator
   - URL: https://www.enrollmedicare.com/calculators/irmaa
   - Strengths: simple calculator, clear 2024 MAGI prompt for 2026 premiums, bracket table directly under the tool, AGI plus tax-exempt interest explanation.

2. US Tax Tools IRMAA Calculator
   - URL: https://ustax.tools/irmaa-calculator/
   - Strengths: strong calculator network with related Roth conversion, RMD, Social Security, 401(k), and tax calculators.

3. Medicare on Main IRMAA Tool
   - URL: https://medicareonmain.com/tools/irmaa
   - Strengths: clean result cards for Part B surcharge, total Part B premium, Part D surcharge, and annual impact.

4. MyMedicareFit IRMAA Calculator
   - URL: https://mymedicarefit.com/irmaa-calculator
   - Strengths: beginner-friendly framing, clear explanation of which income year to use.

5. FinanceWonk 2026 IRMAA Guide
   - URL: https://www.financewonk.com/references/medicare-irmaa
   - Strengths: strong planning content around cliff effect, MAGI, two-year lookback, spouse-adjusted totals, and distance to next bracket.

6. Binfy IRMAA Calculator
   - URL: https://www.binfy.com/irmaa_calculator.php
   - Strengths: utility depth, premium-year selection, prior-year calculations, spouse inclusion, detailed definitions.

7. IRMAA Solutions
   - URL: https://www.irmaasolutions.com/
   - Strengths: monetization structure with pricing/account navigation and professional-service positioning.

8. USA Salary Tools Medicare Calculator
   - URL: https://usasalarytools.com/medicare-calculator
   - Strengths: broad Medicare calculator covering premiums, IRMAA, Medicare tax, penalties, eligibility, and long FAQ content.

9. IRMAA-Aware Roth Conversion Calculator
   - URL: https://financial-advisors-for-roth-conversion.com/irmaa-roth-conversion-calculator/
   - Strengths: sharp high-intent hook: maximum Roth conversion before each IRMAA cliff.

10. Senior Media Hub IRMAA Calculator
    - URL: https://seniormediahub.com/tools/irmaa-calculator/
    - Strengths: simple SEO page with plain-language threshold framing.

## What Competitors Do Better

- Faster above-the-fold result flow: several pages let users enter income and immediately see Part B, Part D, and annual impact.
- More utility depth: Binfy supports premium years, spouse inclusion, and prior-year calculations.
- Stronger planning language: FinanceWonk and Roth-focused competitors explain cliffs and bracket-room decisions clearly.
- Better calculator ecosystem: US Tax Tools links IRMAA to Roth, RMD, Social Security, 401(k), and tax calculators.
- Clearer monetization pages: IRMAA Solutions makes the professional-service path obvious.

## Gaps We Can Fill

- Build an IRMAA decision tool, not only a surcharge lookup.
- Answer high-intent questions directly:
  - How much Roth conversion can I do before the next IRMAA bracket?
  - Will both spouses pay this surcharge?
  - Which income year matters?
  - Can I appeal with SSA-44?
  - What income actually counts toward Medicare MAGI?
  - What happens after a home sale, RMD, consulting income, or IRA withdrawal?
- Combine calculator output, plain-English guidance, sponsor inventory, and lead capture in one senior-friendly workflow.
