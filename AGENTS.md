# AI Coordinator Notes

Mission: Build an IRMAA Surcharge Warning Calculator for adults approaching or using Medicare. The product helps users understand whether income events such as Roth conversions, RMDs, home sales, capital gains, pensions, or part-time work may push them into higher Medicare premium brackets.

Audience: Primarily US adults age 55+, retirees, near-retirees, financial caregivers, and adult children helping parents. Tone should be calm, trustworthy, plain English, and not fear-based.

Positioning: This is not a generic Medicare article. It is a decision-protection tool: "Before you trigger extra Medicare premiums, check your numbers."

Monetization paths:
- Medicare education / plan-comparison affiliates
- Financial advisor lead-gen
- Retirement tax planning affiliates
- Display ads on supporting educational pages

SEO wedge:
- Do not start by trying to rank for "Medicare" broadly.
- Target intent around IRMAA thresholds, Roth conversions, RMDs, capital gains, home sales, and Medicare premium surprises.
- Build calculator-first pages with helpful supporting articles.

Compliance notes:
- Avoid implying personalized tax, legal, investment, or Medicare enrollment advice.
- Show assumptions clearly.
- Link to official Medicare / SSA / IRS sources where rules and thresholds matter.
- Include a clear disclaimer that estimates are educational.


Unified design strategy:
- This is one site with page-type templates, not three separate versions.
- Calculator and homepage pages use the Guided Financial Planner template.
- Bracket and official-reference pages use the Calm Civic Utility template.
- Blog and SEO explainer pages use the Editorial Medicare Guide template.
- Keep ad placements clearly labeled as Advertisement and visually quiet.
- Do not let ad inventory interrupt the calculator workflow or displace the primary answer.
