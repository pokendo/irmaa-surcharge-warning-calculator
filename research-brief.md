# Research Brief: IRMAA Surcharge Warning Calculator

## Why This May Work

IRMAA is a high-anxiety, high-cost Medicare issue for older adults. A small income change can move a user into a higher premium bracket. Users often discover this only after a Roth conversion, RMD, home sale, or capital gain.

The opportunity is not a generic "Medicare calculator." The opportunity is a focused warning tool for retirees and near-retirees who want to avoid expensive surprises.

## Evidence From Prior Research

- Public Semrush snippets surfaced "IRMAA brackets 2026" around 12.1k monthly searches with commercial value.
- Medicare Part B premium content is covered by major finance publishers, which proves demand.
- Existing tools include PolicyRisk, IRMAA Solutions, advisor calculators, and explainer articles from Kiplinger, NerdWallet, and wealth-management firms.

## Competitor Types

- Finance publishers: NerdWallet, Kiplinger.
- Wealth advisors: Savant Wealth and similar firms.
- Calculator/tools: PolicyRisk Medicare premium calculator, IRMAA Solutions.
- Official references: Medicare.gov, SSA.gov.

## Room To Compete

Head terms are not easy, but the long-tail is attractive:

- IRMAA Roth conversion calculator
- Will Roth conversion trigger IRMAA
- RMD Medicare premium calculator
- Home sale Medicare premium increase
- Capital gains IRMAA calculator
- Medicare surcharge income calculator
- How much income before IRMAA

The product should be calculator-first, updated yearly, and built around income-event scenarios rather than static bracket tables.

## Risks

- Rules change yearly, so freshness matters.
- Medical/financial-adjacent content requires trust signals.
- Affiliate partnerships must be handled carefully to preserve neutrality.

## Next Research Tasks

- Pull exact keyword metrics from Ahrefs/Semrush/LowFruits if available.
- Capture SERP screenshots for the top 20 target keywords.

## Chosen Design Direction

The site should use one cohesive design system, not three separate site versions. The selected approach is page-type based:

- Guided Financial Planner for the calculator and homepage, because users need a reassuring step-by-step flow when checking a high-anxiety income decision.
- Calm Civic Utility for bracket tables and official-reference pages, because table-heavy information needs clarity, restraint, and source-like credibility.
- Editorial Medicare Guide for blog and SEO explainers, because educational pages need readable article flow, related-guide navigation, and tasteful ad inventory.

This split lets the site match user intent without fragmenting the brand.
