Privacy Policy

Last updated June 6, 2026. This page explains the information IRMAA Check currently collects and why.

Calculator information

Calculator entries are used in your browser to produce estimates. When you use the site, limited calculator context and interaction events may be stored with first-party analytics so we can understand which tools are useful. Do not enter Social Security numbers, tax documents, account numbers, or other sensitive identifiers.

Newsletter signups and inquiries

If you request updates, we store the email address, signup source, page path, and limited calculator context associated with the signup. Sponsor inquiry forms store the company, contact name, email, requested placement, message, and attribution source you provide.

Analytics, referrals, and advertising

We record basic first-party events such as page views, signup sources, and sponsor-link clicks. URL campaign parameters and the referring website may be stored for attribution. The site may add third-party advertising or analytics services later; this policy will be updated before those services are enabled.

Storage, sharing, and choices

Site records are stored in the project-owned PocketBase service. We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers needed to operate the site or when required by law. To ask about, correct, or request deletion of information you submitted, use the contact page.

Changes

This policy may change as the site adds services. The updated date at the top will change when material updates are published.
