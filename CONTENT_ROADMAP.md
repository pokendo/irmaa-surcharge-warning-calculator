## Ad and Sponsor Placement Rules

- Put the answer first. Do not place ads before the first useful answer.
- Use clearly labeled `Advertisement` blocks.
- Best early placements:
  - Inline sponsor block after the first answer.
  - Right-rail sponsor on desktop.
  - Bottom sponsor block after calculator CTA.
- Do not use aggressive Medicare plan, insurance, tax, legal, or investment advice language.
- For Google AdSense readiness, keep pages content-rich, original, useful, and easy to navigate before applying.
