Sponsor FAQ

Do you publish medical or financial advice?
No. IRMAA Check publishes educational estimates and planning context only. Sponsor copy must also avoid tax, legal, investment, Medicare enrollment, or insurance advice.
Can sponsors target a specific planning topic?
Yes. The best early fit is matching a sponsor resource to a scenario page, such as Roth conversions, RMDs, home sales, capital gains, or Medicare MAGI education.
Do sponsor placements have to be labeled?
Yes. Sponsor placements stay clearly labeled as Advertisement so readers understand what is editorial content and what is paid sponsorship.
